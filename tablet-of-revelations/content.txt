Tablet of Revelations

We'll ask 8 questions, and the top panel contains 10 holes which line up with a 10x10 grid of letters on the lower panel.
Letters along the diagonals can't be used.
Each hole is unique across all 8 transforms.
Each cell is exactly 10mm square.
Holes can't be adjacent to each other.
